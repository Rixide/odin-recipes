Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic baked Italian dish made by layering wide, flat pasta with a rich, savory sauce, various meats, and a decadent blend of cheeses.

ingredients

  • Pasta1 box (12–16 oz) lasagna noodles (or oven-ready sheets)
  • Meat & Tomato Sauce1 lb ground beef1 lb bulk Italian sausage1 yellow onion, diced2–3 cloves garlic, minced1 tablespoon olive oil2 cans (28 oz each) tomato sauce or marinara sauce1 can (6 oz) tomato paste1 tablespoon Italian seasoning (or a mix of basil, oregano, and thyme)Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Cheese Mixture15 oz ricotta cheese (or small-curd cottage cheese)1 large egg (to bind the cheese)3-4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese1 cup grated Parmesan cheese¼ cup fresh parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. Prep the Cheese & PastaMix the filling: In a medium bowl, stir together the ricotta cheese, egg, chopped parsley, and half of the Parmesan cheese.Boil the noodles: Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions, drain, and lay them flat on foil to prevent sticking (skip this if using oven-ready noodles).
  2. Assemble the LayersBase layer: Spread 1 cup of the meat sauce across the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.First layer: Lay down 3 to 4 noodles to cover the sauce. Spread a portion of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, then sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan.Repeat: Add another layer of meat sauce, then noodles, ricotta, and mozzarella. Repeat until you run out of ingredients, finishing with a generous layer of mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  3. Bake to PerfectionCover and bake: Cover the dish loosely with aluminum foil (so it doesn't touch the cheese). Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes.Brown the top: Remove the foil and bake for another 25 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.Rest: Let the lasagna rest for 15 minutes before slicing so it holds its shape.
