Classic baked Ziti Recipe

Description

Baked Ziti gives you all the flavors of a meat lasagna but requires zero tedious layering. The tubular pasta traps the rich tomato meat sauce inside, while pockets of creamy ricotta and melted mozzarella melt evenly throughout the dish.

ingredients

  • The Pasta: 16 oz ziti (or penne) pasta.
  • The Meat Sauce: 1 lb Italian sausage (or ground beef), 1 diced yellow onion, 2 minced garlic cloves, 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce, 1 can (14 oz) tomato sauce, 1 tsp Italian seasoning.
  • The Béchamel Sauce: ¼ cup butter, ¼ cup flour, 2.5 cups warm milk, ¼ tsp nutmeg, 2 egg yolks, 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese.The Cheese: 15 oz ricotta cheese, 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese, 1 egg.

    Steps

    1. Boil Pasta: Cook ziti pasta in salted water until just underdone (it finishes cooking in the oven). Drain.
    2. Brown Meat: Brown the sausage with onions and garlic in a large skillet. Drain fat, then stir in marinara sauce, tomato sauce, and Italian seasoning. Simmer for 10 minutes.
    3. Mix Cheese: In a medium bowl, stir together the ricotta, egg, ½ cup of mozzarella, and half of the Parmesan.
    4. Combine: Toss the cooked pasta, the cheese mixture, and 2 cups of the meat sauce together in a large bowl until well coated.
    5. Assemble: Pour half the pasta mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish. Spoon half of the remaining meat sauce over it and sprinkle with 1 cup of mozzarella. Repeat with the remaining pasta, sauce, and mozzarella.
    6. Bake: Bake uncovered at 375°F (190°C) for 25–30 minutes until the cheese is bubbling and golden at the edges.
